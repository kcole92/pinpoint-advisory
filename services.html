What We Do

We offer a focused suite of services designed to keep your financial foundation solid and your decisions well-informed. Choose the level of support that fits where your business is today.

Strategic Guidance

Every business is different. We provide personalized guidance tailored to your goals, your industry, and the complexity of what you're managing, from clean, audit-ready books to full fractional controller support.

Core

CPA-overseen bookkeeping: accurate books and organized records, reviewed and interpreted by a CPA rather than handed off to a generalist. Built for businesses that need a solid, reliable foundation before they need deeper analysis.

  • Data entry and transaction categorization, reviewed under CPA oversight
  • Bank reconciliations
  • Monthly financial statements
  • Tax-ready financials at year end
  • Quarterly 30-minute call to review your reports and answer questions

Growth

Everything in Core, plus the analysis and reporting cadence a business needs once its financials start driving real decisions: budgeting, more frequent reporting, and a closer read on what the numbers mean.

  • Everything in Core
  • Custom report generation and financial data analysis
  • Budget preparation
  • Quarterly, monthly, and annual financial statements
  • Monthly 30-minute call to review your reports and discuss your numbers

Advisory

Advisory is our flagship tier, built for businesses that have outgrown their traditional bookkeeper but aren't ready for a full-time controller. This is technical accounting judgment and fractional controller-level guidance, delivered as an ongoing partnership rather than a monthly close.

  • Everything in Growth
  • Fractional controller services
  • Cash flow forecasting, evaluation, and profitability improvement strategies
  • Guidance aligned to your long-term financial goals
  • KPI tracking and performance monitoring
  • Monthly 60-minute advisory call

Simple & Straightforward Pricing

We believe you should always know what you're paying and what you're getting. That's why Pinpoint Advisory offers fixed monthly packages with no hourly billing, so your costs are predictable and your engagement is clearly defined from day one.

Our Core, Growth, and Advisory packages are designed to meet businesses where they are. Because every business is different, pricing is tailored to the complexity of your accounting, your reporting needs, and scope of services.
