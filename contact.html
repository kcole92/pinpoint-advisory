Frequently Asked Questions

Do you work with businesses outside of Georgia?

Yes, we work with clients remotely and are not limited by location. As long as you have a reliable internet connection and are ready to get organized, we can work together.

What if my books are behind or disorganized?

That's exactly the kind of situation we help with. You don't need to have everything in order before reaching out. Getting you cleaned up and caught up is part of what we do.

How long does it take to get started?

As quickly or as deliberately as your business needs. Once we sign an engagement letter, we will collaborate with you to set an onboarding pace that works for your schedule. We'll walk you through everything you need to get us set up with access to your accounts.

Do I need to know anything about accounting to work with you?

Not at all. In fact, that's the point. We handle the technical side and explain what matters in plain language so you can focus on running your business.

Which package is right for me?

We'll help you figure that out during your free consultation. Every business is different, and we'd rather recommend the right fit than push you toward something you don't need.
