Frequently Asked Questions

Which tier is right for us?

We'll help you figure that out during your free consultation. Every business is different, and we'd rather recommend the right fit than push you toward something you don't need.

Can you work alongside our existing bookkeeper or accounting staff?

Yes. If you already have someone handling day-to-day bookkeeping, we can provide the technical oversight, financial reporting, and judgment calls that sit above that layer of work. If you don't have that in place yet, we can integrate foundational bookkeeping into any of our tiers as all services are customizable.

Can you prepare our taxes?

We don't offer tax preparation. Our focus is technical accounting, financial reporting, and advisory. We work with a number of trusted referral partners who specialize in tax, and we're happy to make an introduction so your tax and accounting stay well-coordinated.

Do you work with businesses outside of Georgia?

Yes. We work with clients remotely and aren't limited by location, provided we have secure access to your financial systems and reporting tools.

How long does it take to get started?

Once we sign an engagement letter, we'll work with you to set an onboarding pace that fits your business, and walk you through everything needed to get us set up with access to your accounts.
