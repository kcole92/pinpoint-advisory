About Pinpoint Advisory

We help small business owners understand their numbers — and use them.

Clarity Where It Counts

Pinpoint Advisory was founded on a simple belief: growing businesses deserve the same level of financial rigor and judgment that large companies take for granted, without carrying a large company's overhead.

We provide accurate, organized books and records. More importantly, we help you understand what they mean. Whether you're trying to manage cash flow, evaluate profitability, or plan your next move, we translate your financial data into clear, actionable guidance.

Pinpoint Advisory is led by a CPA who brings a foundational background in Big 4 assurance and expertise in Fortune 500 financial reporting. That background is what allows us to handle the judgment calls, not just the data entry, for businesses whose accounting has outgrown a traditional bookkeeper.

We Partner With:

Professional Service Firms

Law firms, engineering and architecture firms, marketing agencies, and medical practices. Partner or project driven businesses where revenue recognition, utilization tracking, and accounting judgment matter more than transaction volume.

Businesses Preparing for What's Next

Companies approaching an audit, investment, or banking relationship, as well as firms who need accurate financial data to manage internal profit-sharing equitably, maximize realization rates, and confidently project cash flow as they scale.

Businesses Outgrowing Their Bookkeeper

You have someone handling data entry, but no one who can interpret the numbers, catch technical issues before they become problems, or speak as a peer to your CPA firm or lender.

Our Values

Clarity over complexity. Financial information should be straightforward and useful, not confusing or overwhelming. We translate the numbers into language that actually makes sense for your business.

Accuracy you can count on. The foundation of good decisions is good data. We take the details seriously so you never have to question whether your books reflect reality.

Rigor from the top. We bring the same quality, precision, and depth found in Fortune 500 financial reporting and Big 4 assurance work to every engagement.

Relationships over transactions. We're not just here to close the books each month. We're here to be a trusted resource you can lean on when the decisions get harder.

Our Mission

To make financial clarity accessible to every business. We believe that when business owners truly understand their numbers, they make better decisions, build stronger businesses, and worry less. Our job is to give you that understanding, every step of the way.
